Use cases

Read while you keep doing the actual task.

Peekline is for moments when text should stay near your focus, but a full-size window would be too much. The strip works well for coding, demoing, live presenting, writing, customer support, or reviewing structured notes.

Developer flow

Keep prompts and reference notes visible while coding in the main editor.

Presentation mode

Use a calm teleprompter strip for talks, recordings, and walkthroughs.

Writing sessions

Pin outlines, edits, or research bullets above the draft without covering the page.

How it works

Three steps to a cleaner reading surface.

1

Open the text you want nearby: notes, markdown, script fragments, or prompts.

2

Place it into Peekline so the notch area becomes a slim reading strip.

3

Keep working in your actual app while Peekline handles only the reference text.
